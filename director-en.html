Néstor Andrenacci (Director)

Director, teacher and singer since 1973.

In addition to GCC-Grupo de Canto Coral, he directs the Choir of the Argentine National Bank, Trilce (the choir of the Office of Books and the Promotion of Reading for the Government of the City of Buenos Aires) the Cantoría del Socorro and the Francisco Guerrero Madrigal Group. He co-directs the Buenos Aires Orfeón.

From 1989 to 2010 he was Chair of the Department of Choral Direction in the College of Arts and Musical Sciences at the Catholic University of Argentina.

He has performed in Belgium, Brazil, Canada, Chile, Denmark, Ecuador, France, Germany, Israel, Mexico, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, the United Kingdom and Uruguay.

The Argentine House of Representatives has formally recognized Maestro Andrenacci for his many audio and video recordings. Nine CDs among these are with GCC-Grupo de Canto Coral.
